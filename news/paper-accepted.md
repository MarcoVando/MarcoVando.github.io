---
title: "Paper accepted at Chemistry of Materials"
date: "Jun 2026"
---

Our paper on **flexible metal-organic frameworks** has been accepted at Chemistry of Materials! 

This work demonstrates a new class of multi-responsive MOFs that undergo structural transformations upon guest adsorption, temperature, and pressure changes. The study combines in situ synchrotron X-ray diffraction with computational modeling to elucidate the structural dynamics at the molecular level.
